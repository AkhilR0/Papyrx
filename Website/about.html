What is Papyrx?

Papyrx bridges the gap between community and individuals through reading. Many people in their busy, day to day lives, often say that they don't have the time to read. Thats where Papyrx comes in. We offer simplified book summaries of your favorite books through reccomendation algorithms and give you daily notifications.

Not only do you become enriched through Papyrx through reading, other people who have read the same books as you can submit their own summaries of books to get volunteer hours. This means that the community gets to help each other out while also helping other individuals to enjoy their favorite books.
